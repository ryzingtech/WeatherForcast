Understanding Climate Change

About Climate Change

Climate change refers to long-term alterations in temperature, precipitation, wind patterns, and other elements of the Earth's climate system. It is largely driven by human activities such as burning fossil fuels, deforestation, and industrial processes that increase greenhouse gases in the atmosphere.

Learn more about the science behind climate change and how it is affecting our world.

Impacts of Climate Change

Climate change has significant impacts on the environment, human health, agriculture, and economies worldwide. Rising sea levels, increased frequency of extreme weather events, and changes in biodiversity are just a few of the challenges we face.

Understand how these changes affect us and what we can do to adapt and mitigate these impacts.

Solutions to Combat Climate Change

Addressing climate change requires a collective effort from governments, businesses, and individuals. Solutions include transitioning to renewable energy sources, improving energy efficiency, and adopting sustainable agricultural practices.
